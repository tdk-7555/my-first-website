About Tirmizi Trips

About Me - The Founder:

My name is Mohammad Osman. I'm a British Pakistani/Mauritian, born and raised in the UK. Growing up, I would be travelling to both my Home countries often. However, I had never really been on a proper holiday to any other countries.

As I got older, I started to travel to other places too and discovered my love for travel. I realised I loved sight-seeing and learning about other cultures. So far, I would say my favourite destinations have been: California, Bursa and New York.
